Technology has reshaped the way we trade our valueable resources. Airbnb turns our idle room into sweet cash flow, Lyft makes it easy to cruise in the beautiful city while making a few extra bucks. More importantly, they make the process social and fun, we not only exchange our resources for money, but also for fun and friends! But how about the most valueable resources we process? Our professional skills that we spend years in school to learn and even more years in the industry to sharpen?

Skillore is the that place for you skillset. By simply logging in with your Linkedin account, you are connected to thousands of opportunities to help other people with the tasks specifically matched to your skills. By completing the task, you are not only rewarded by cash, but also by positive review on Skillore and precious endorsement on your Linkedin profile. It's the best way to make your skill truely valueable, makes it bring you money, network and opportunity.
